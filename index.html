About AGRIONE

Built for farms that need surgical precision, not broad-spectrum guesswork.

Traditional spraying blankets entire fields, even when problems start at the leaf or row level. AGRIONE replaces that waste with a data-driven system that sees earlier, decides faster, and applies only where action is needed.

The Problem

Overuse of pesticides and inefficient field response are eroding margins.

Farmers often react with full-field treatment because the exact location and severity of crop stress are hard to identify in real time. That means more chemical use, higher cost, and less sustainable operations.

  • Blanket spraying wastes input and labor.
  • Late detection allows disease to spread before intervention.
  • Manual scouting misses hidden patterns across large acreage.
The Solution

AGRIONE connects AI detection to autonomous drone action in one loop.

Imaging, models, and robotic actuation work together as a closed feedback system. The platform detects anomalies, identifies target plants, aligns the gimbal path, and executes precision spray with mission-level visibility.

  • AI identifies disease signatures and stress hotspots.
  • Drone and gimbal systems align to precise coordinates.
  • Mission telemetry keeps operators informed and in control.
How It Works

An end-to-end precision workflow from image capture to targeted spray.

AGRIONE continuously processes field imagery, recognizes actionable plant-level issues, then translates that intelligence into controlled spray execution with geospatial context and operator oversight.

01 CAM

Camera Capture

RGB and multispectral imagery collect canopy detail across rows, blocks, and stress zones.

02 AI

AI Detection

Models inspect the live feed for disease signatures, nutrient stress, and anomaly patterns.

03 ID

Target Identification

The system isolates affected plants and calculates treatment priority by location and severity.

04 NAV

Gimbal + Drone Alignment

Flight controls and gimbal orientation reposition the spray system over exact treatment points.

05 SPRAY

Precision Spray

Targeted pesticide application is delivered only where action is required, plant by plant.
